**Uber Eats Feature Masterlist**

Users increasingly want delivery that fits their specific diet, lifestyle, schedule, and budget, and the broad platforms are structurally too generic to serve that well.

Halal-only delivery network:	Underserved dietary community with strong loyalty once trust is built  
Corporate office lunch ordering: Recurring B2B demand; group ordering and invoicing are dealbreakers if missing  
Gym / fitness meal tracking app: Auto-tracks calories and macros — ties delivery directly to a health goal  
Hyperlocal dark kitchen aggregator:	Owns one city extremely well instead of spreading thin nationally  
Senior subscription meal service: Recurring revenue; dietary restriction handling is a genuine differentiator.

## Two Types of Upselling

**Type 1 — AI Side Dish Suggestion (Customer-facing)**

* Customer adds Chicken Tikka Masala → AI suggests "Would you also like Garlic Naan for £2.50?"

* Based on real order combination data — not random suggestions

* Toggle on/off per restaurant. Revenue impact tracked and shown to restaurant.

**Type 2 — Remaining Stock Visibility (Staff-facing)**

* Kitchen or admin screen shows: "Chicken Roast: 10 remaining" — not visible to customers

* Staff know what to prioritise selling before preparing a new batch

* Restaurant close reminder: system lists all out-of-stock items and asks if they should be re-enabled next day
